How to predict

Every Round of 32 match is already filled. Pick who advances two ways: drag a team onto its W… placeholder in the next round, or simply click a team to push it forward. In the Final, click a finalist to crown your champion. The × clears a pick. Your predictions auto-save in this browser, so refreshing the page won't lose them — and Export JSON backs them up to a bracket-data.json file you can reload anytime.
