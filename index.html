How to predict

Every Round of 32 match is already filled. Advance teams two ways: click a team to push it forward, or drag it onto its W… placeholder in the next round. The third-place play-off fills itself the moment both semi-final winners are chosen — the two losers drop straight in, and you can click one to award the bronze 🥉. In the Final, click a finalist to crown your champion 🏆. The × clears a pick. Picks auto-save in this browser; Export JSON backs them up to a bracket-data.json file you can reload anytime.
